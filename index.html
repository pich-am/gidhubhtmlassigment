Phnom Penh, January 17, 2022: Minister of Post and Telecommunications Chea Vandeth presided over the inauguration ceremony of the AUPP Technology Center (ATC) of the American University of Phnom Penh. Also present at the inauguration were leaderships from various ministries, technology companies, educational institutions, as well as many distinguished guests. The ATC is a technology center launched after the Innovation Center of the Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology and other technology centers. The ATC is an international standard tech center that can contribute to the development of the digital sector in Cambodia through research and development as well as innovation to support the implementation of the digital government and digital economy policies. In addition, ATC will provide opportunities for digital start-ups in Cambodia to receive support, consultation from experts, marketing, product sales, and funding in order to strengthen and expand their businesses to another level.
